Programme Structure

Our internships run for three months and are designed to accelerate your skills. We offer two tracks to accommodate different lifestyles:

Full‑Time Interns (Weekday Onsite)

Full‑time interns are immersed in our Vijayawada office Monday through Friday. They work alongside our core engineering and design teams on live projects, attend daily stand‑ups, and participate in workshops covering Python, data science, machine learning, web development, user experience and AI ethics. This intensive track is ideal for students who can commit fully and want to experience the rhythm of a professional studio.

Part‑Time Interns (Weekend Online)

For working professionals and students balancing other responsibilities, we offer a part‑time programme conducted online on weekends. Live sessions focus on conceptual foundations and project work, while asynchronous assignments allow you to practice during the week. You’ll build a portfolio piece by the end of the course, guided by mentors who understand the challenges of juggling work and study.

Regardless of the track, participants develop technical expertise, soft skills and an entrepreneurial mindset within a supportive community. By the end of three months, you will have built and deployed projects using AI, web development frameworks and collaborative tools, preparing you for the next step in your career.
